ABOUT THE INSTITUTE

Since its inception in 2004, Pranveer Singh Institute of Technology (PSIT), Kanpur has been a frontrunner in the field of education with numerous achievements to its name. PSIT believes in strengthening the foundation of the students. Academic research based on rigorous pedagogy equips our students to face challenges in the real world. The institute prepares students to be ethical leaders in a diverse and complex world. PSIT offers various courses in Engineering, Business Management and Computer Applications. All departments, with their dynamic community of students and distinguished faculty, offer premium undergraduate and postgraduate programs. State of the art facilities and a sprawling 80 acres campus nestled amid sylvan surroundings accelerate the learning process.

The PSIT campus is an architectural marvel. Designed as the best environment-friendly campus in North India, we are committed to providing our students a clean and healthy environment. A 25,000 Ltr RO Plant, a 1350 KW Power Plant and a three-chambered Vermicompost Plant reaffirm our commitment to the cause.

Our infrastructure, which includes an auditorium, modern workshops, high-tech labs, spacious seminar halls and well stocked libraries, can compete with the best campuses. Our fleet of 38 air-conditioned CNG buses ensures an efficient transportation network.

The institute provides a stimulating environment for intellectual development, free-thinking, and personal growth. The academic setup challenges its students with dynamic learning opportunities. We equip them with the skills, insights, attitudes, and practical experiences to take up responsibilities in their careers and life.
